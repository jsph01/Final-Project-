Uploading Image or Text

To upload an image ot text, on the Customize page, click insert image, then once the upload window appears, click the choose file button to locate the file on your computer that you want to upload. After click upload.

Please note file uploads may take anywhere from a few seconds to a few minutes depending on your connection speed and file size.

Saving

To save your design on Customize page, click the Save button. If you are not logged in, you will be prompted to do so now or create an account if you do not already have one. Once you are logged in, click save, enter a name for your design, and then click the Save Design button. That's it! Your design is now saved for you to retrieve at a later time.
